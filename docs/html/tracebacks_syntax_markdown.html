TabError¶

Python exception¶

TabError: inconsistent use of tabs and spaces in indentation¶

A TabError indicates that you have used both spaces and tab characters to indent your code. This is not allowed in Python. Indenting your code means to have block of codes aligned vertically by inserting either spaces or tab characters at the beginning of lines. Python’s recommendation is to always use spaces to indent your code.
